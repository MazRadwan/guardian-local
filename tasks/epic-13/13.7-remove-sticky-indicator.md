**Story 13.7 – Remove Sticky Indicator & Visibility Hook Cleanup**

Goal: Remove the slim “questionnaire ready” sticky indicator bar and its visibility tracking. The inline questionnaire bubble is now the sole status surface.

Scope:
- Frontend only (apps/web).
- Likely touch:

  - `apps/web/src/hooks/useQuestionnaireCardVisibility.ts` (remove if only used for the sticky bar)

  - `apps/web/src/components/chat/MessageList.tsx` (ensure no dangling slot/props for indicator)
  - Tests referencing the indicator under `apps/web/src/components/chat/__tests__/`

- Remove rendering of the sticky indicator above the composer.

- Do not affect questionnaire bubble functionality, states, or the stepper; this is a cleanup only.
- Keep selectors/testids stable for remaining elements; remove indicator-specific testids.

Non-goals:

- Download durability or rehydration changes.
- “Guardian is thinking” banner removal (handled separately).

Testing:

- Verify chat layout snapshots (MessageList/ChatInterface) pass without the indicator elements.

Deliverables:
- Sticky indicator removed, visibility hook cleaned up, and tests updated accordingly.

## What This Epic Does

Extracts `generateTitleIfNeeded()` and `updateScoringTitle()` from MessageHandler.ts into a dedicated `TitleUpdateService` in the infrastructure layer. Second extraction in the multi-epic MessageHandler decomposition.

## Why This Epic Exists

1. MessageHandler.ts is 1,319 LOC (limit: 300)
2. Title generation is isolated (single dep, fire-and-forget, zero coupling to streaming/tool loop)
3. The regeneration bug (Epic 34) showed tightly-coupled features cascade failures
4. Each extraction reduces risk surface for future changes

## What NOT to Touch

- Any MessageHandler method other than `generateTitleIfNeeded()` and `updateScoringTitle()`
- ConsultToolLoopService (Epic 34)
- ScoringHandler, QuestionnaireHandler
- File upload / export flows
